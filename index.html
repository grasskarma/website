Common Questions

How much does Grass Karma cost?

Grass Karma will be free for basic roster coordination. Premium features like professional service booking will have a small service fee that's split among participants.

What if my neighbours don't want to participate?

No worries! Even 2-3 participating households can benefit from coordination. You can always invite more neighbours as they see the benefits.

How do you handle payments for professional services?

We integrate with secure payment providers to automatically split and collect payments. No more chasing neighbours for cash!

When will Grass Karma be available?

We're launching in 2025! Early access members will be first to try the app and help shape its development.
