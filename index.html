🌱 Grass Karma

Australia's first app to help neighbours collaborate on mowing their nature strips. Share the work, save money, and make your street look great together.

How it works:

  • 📅 Create a street-wide mowing roster with your neighbours
  • 🤝 Team up and take turns — or
  • 💸 Pitch in and pay a local or pro to mow the entire street
